ABOUT US

Shalu Gupta Advocate is a full-service law firm, based in Kolkata, that offers global standards of legal services. The firm was established in 2009 and the founder, Ms. Shalu Gupta, has had an enriching professional experience of 12 years in the field of law. Since its establishment, the firm has offered exceptional legal solutions in civil, property, banking and matrimonial matters. The firm has a dedicated team of professionals, including lawyers, technical experts and human resources with specific industry expertise to meet the most complex requirements of clients. Committed to resolve and minimize the legal obligations of clients, the firm offers the simplest and the most effective solutions, with the primary objective of making the community understand and abide by the laws under the Indian Constitution. The firm extends its services to clients in West Bengal as well as in Ludhiana, Chandigarh and Delhi. India continues to be an ever-evolving country, in terms of legal policies and regulations. Ms. Shalu Gupta plays an instrumental role in helping clients with legal assistance and keeping them informed about the changes so that they remain legally compliant. Coupled with a strong ethical foundation, acumen as well as integrity, the firm is well-equipped to help clients with a broad spectrum of legal solutions.

WHAT WE DO

We offer unmatched legal expertise across a wide range of legal areas. We have established a reputation for being effective, dependable and prompt in our services. The range of our services are following

Land And Property
Land and property disputes often turn into long-term mental and physical hassles. The real estate market is nothing less than quick sand. If you know your way out, you are sorted. If you are inexperienced, it will pull you in and you will keep falling into a bottomless pit of anxiety and apprehension. While land and property fraud cases are rampant in India, there are effective legal ways to solve the most stubborn ones as well.
Armed Forces Matters
We can represent a wide range of military clientele and we are well-versed to cater across all ranks.
Debt Settlement
We help banks in matters of debt settlement by settling on a reduced balance agreed upon by the debtor and the bank.
Divorce
A divorce could be a lonely and painful fight. We can hold your hand through it and take all the legal responsibilities at our end.
Child Custody
The security of your child is our responsibility. We will help you sail through this traumatic phase as comfortably as possible and will ensure that the end decision is in your favour
Domestic Violence
Are you a victim of domestic violence? Let us help you by acquiring a restraining order from court, filing a domestic violence lawsuit and assist you through the entire legal process.
Arbitration Proceedings
We have successfully obtained arbitration awards for our clients, in matters of high-stake commercial disputes.
Lok Adalat Matters
If you wish to address/settle your disputes amicably at a pre-litigation stage, then we can help get your case referred to the Lok Adalat.
Labour Rights
We will help you to sail through the complexity of labour laws so that you can focus on your business. We will rule out the risks and losses that often challenge the business world
Searching
We help to search commercial industrial and urban property while clearly establishing their status with proper documentation.
Mutation
You can take our legal advice to keep your property record clean. We can also assist in re-checking revenue records of purchased or sold property.
Registration
If you are disposing a property, we will help you to register all documents related to lease, will, gift, sale or transfer so that you comply to Section 17 of Indian Registration Act, 1908
Debt Recovery Tribunal
We help banks and other financial institutions in faster debt recovery from their customers.
Injunction Order
We can file and defend your injunction suit so that your right is always asserted against a third-party mischief.
Consumer Disputes
We will also defend your cause if you, as a consumer, are being deprived of your consumer laws by unfair and misleading trade practice
Assessment
If you feel that your property has either been over-valued or under-valued by the property assessor, then you could take our help to issue an appeal or a law suit for reconsideration
